Sobre Mí

PhD in Comparative Politics. Previously, I worked as a research fellow at the Universidad de Salamanca withi the project 'Political preferences and populist attitudes: changes in political systems after the Great Recession. My research is focused on analysing new radical political parties through a majority of methodological approaches, such as QCA, quantitative methods, and discourse analysys. My work has been published in relevant academic journals, like /Journal of Contemporary European Studies/, /Journal of Political Ideologies/, and /European Politics and Society/
